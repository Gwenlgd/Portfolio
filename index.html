PROJECT

HEIKO

Understand what your body is telling you and find the balance!

- Enter your meals, the effects on your body and let Heiko explain what is going on ! -

During my two-week final project at Le Wagon bootcamp, we developed Heiko - an app that helps users who are struggling with food allergies or reactions.

Heiko allows users to input their meals and track the physical effects and mood changes they experience. The app's algorithm provides users with personalized summaries on how various foods affect their bodies, enabling them to make more informed decisions.

By focusing on the effects of food on the body, Heiko offers a unique approach to food diary apps, promoting more balanced lifestyle.
